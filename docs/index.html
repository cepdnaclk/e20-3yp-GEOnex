Smart Surveyor
Site Printing Rover

The Site Printing Rover is a smart land surveying tool designed to automate the stakeout process in land surveying. Equipped with advanced IoT-enabled features, it can mark lines and patterns with precision according to a given plan. This innovation addresses the inefficiencies of traditional methods, making surveying faster, more accurate, and more cost-effective.

Vision

To revolutionize land surveying by providing an affordable, automated tool that ensures precision and efficiency in marking layouts based on pre-defined plans.

Objective

Develop an affordable and efficient IoT-enabled robot for marking lines or patterns on even surfaces using a spray bottle mechanism. This robot will cater to small-scale construction, surveying, and design projects and cost-effectiveness

Applications

  • Construction: Automated stakeouts and layout marking for structures.
  • Design and Planning: Marking pathways or patterns based on CAD plans.
