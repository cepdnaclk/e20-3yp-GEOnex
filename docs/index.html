Smart Surveying Device

The Smart Surveying Device is an innovative solution addressing the inefficiencies in land surveying, boundary marking, and construction layout tasks. It automates stake placement with high precision, reducing both manual labor and project costs.

Vision

To revolutionize land surveying by providing an affordable, automated solution that enhances accuracy and efficiency in surveying and stake out process.

Project Overview

The objective of the Smart Surveying Device is to develop an automated system that takes land coordinates, mapping and places stakes precisely according to preloaded survey data. By addressing the challenges of manual surveying and staking, the device minimizes human error, speeds up layout marking, and reduces overall project costs.

Key Features

  • Automated Stake Placement: Precisely positions stakes based on survey data.
  • Digital Mapping and Reports: Generates detailed reports compatible with CAD, PDF, and other formats.
  • Real-Time Data Visualization: Provides dynamic monitoring of progress and mapped areas.
  • Land Measurments Area calulations and other basic cad funtionality.
